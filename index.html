About Me

Hi, I'm Ange... Welcome to my work-in-progress.

This is an archive of things made and things found. I lost touch with my creative joy for a while... this is the story of finding it again in the grit of a pencil sketch and the story of a vintage find.

Between the walls of my home and the pages of my sketchbook, I'm learning to trade perfection for presence again.
